Room Categories

Choosing Your Room — What Level Is Right for You?

One of the most important Sandals decisions is your room category. Every category includes the full all-inclusive experience — the difference is in room size, location, views, and exclusive perks.

Butler Suite Categories

Top Tier · Ultimate Luxury

Sandals' most exclusive experience — your own personal Butler available around the clock. Your Butler handles restaurant reservations, unpacks your luggage, arranges private beach setups, orders room service, and anticipates your every need. Over-the-water bungalows fall in this category at select resorts.

Best for: Honeymoons, milestone anniversaries, once-in-a-lifetime trips

Club Level Categories

Mid-Premium · Great Value

Access to exclusive Club Sandals lounges with premium food and drinks, priority check-in, and upgraded room amenities. You get a significant step up in the experience without the full Butler price point. Excellent sweet spot for most couples.

Best for: Couples who want extras without the full Butler splurge

Standard Categories

Entry Level · Still Exceptional

Don't let "standard" fool you — every Sandals room is beautifully appointed and includes the full all-inclusive experience. These categories often offer great value and still feature resort views, quality furnishings, and access to all resort amenities.

Best for: Budget-conscious couples who don't want to compromise on the destination

Caribbean Destinations

Where to Go — Island by Island

Each island has a distinct personality — and Sandals selects only the best beach locations on each. Here's a quick guide to help you choose.

Jamaica

Jamaica

Most Sandals resorts are here — great for first-timers. Vibrant culture, lush landscape, excellent beaches. Montego Bay is easiest for flights. Negril has the iconic seven-mile beach. Ocho Rios offers waterfalls and adventure.

Turks & Caicos

Turks & Caicos

Home to Beaches Turks & Caicos — consistently ranked the #1 family resort in the world. Grace Bay Beach is one of the most beautiful in the Caribbean. Calm, clear, shallow water — perfect for families. More expensive but worth it.

St. Lucia

St. Lucia

Arguably the most dramatic island in the Caribbean — volcanic peaks, lush rainforest, and stunning hillside views. Sandals St. Lucia is iconic. Best for couples wanting beauty and romance over party vibes.

Barbados

Barbados

Sophisticated, polished, and a bit more European in feel. Known for excellent dining, beautiful beaches, and a thriving local culture. Sandals Royal Barbados is a premium property worth the investment.

Antigua

Antigua

365 beaches — one for every day of the year. Sandals Grande Antigua is set on Dickenson Bay, one of the island's most beautiful stretches of sand. Excellent for watersports. Quieter and more peaceful than Jamaica.

Grenada · Curaçao · Bahamas

Grenada, Curaçao & Bahamas

Grenada offers spice-island charm and dramatic beauty. Curaçao brings colorful Dutch architecture and world-class diving. Nassau Bahamas is convenient from the US East Coast — perfect for shorter trips.

Best Time to Visit

When to Go to the Caribbean

Caribbean weather is generally warm year-round, but the island chain does have a hurricane season. Here's how to time your trip for the best combination of weather, value, and availability.

January
🟢 Best

Peak season begins. Dry weather, low humidity, no hurricane risk. Book well in advance.

February
🟢 Best

Ideal Caribbean weather. Popular for Valentine's getaways and honeymoons. Book early.

March
🟢 Best

Excellent conditions. Still in peak season. Slightly more crowded due to spring break travelers.

April
🟢 Best

Great weather continues. Toward the end of peak season — some of the best rates start appearing.

May
🔵 Good

Off-season begins. Rates drop significantly. Weather still very good — low hurricane activity.

June
🔵 Good

Early hurricane season begins but risk is still low. Great rates and fewer tourists. Popular for weddings.

July
🟡 Caution

Hurricane season in earnest. Still many calm days — but monitor forecasts. Rates are often very low.

August
🔴 Hurricane Risk

Peak hurricane activity. Sandals offers travel protection — but August carries the most weather uncertainty.

September
🔴 Hurricane Risk

Statistically the most active hurricane month. Lowest rates of the year — but highest risk. Travel insurance essential.

October
🔵 Good

Hurricane season winds down. Weather improves dramatically late in the month. Great value rates still available.

November
🟢 Best

Shoulder season sweet spot — beautiful weather, lower rates than peak season, fewer crowds. Highly recommended.

December
🟢 Best

Peak season returns. Christmas and New Year's see highest demand and rates. Thanksgiving week is excellent value.

Sandals offers their own travel protection plan. Given the Caribbean's hurricane season and the nature of international travel, I always recommend purchasing it. Here's what matters:

  • Trip cancellation and interruption coverage for covered reasons
  • Compare Sandals' plan vs. third-party travel insurance — I help clients make this decision

Food & Dining

Dining at Sandals & Beaches

🍴

Multiple Restaurants Included

Sandals resorts feature anywhere from 5 to 16+ restaurants per property — all included, no reservations required for most. Cuisine ranges from Caribbean to Japanese, Italian, French, and more. You can eat at a different restaurant every night and never pay extra.

🥂

Premium Bar Service

Top-shelf liquors, wines, and beers are included around the clock. Swim-up bars, beach bars, pool bars, lobby bars — all open and included. No drink tickets, no wristband upgrades, no tipping required. This alone is a significant value vs. other resorts.

🌅

Private Dining Experiences

Butler Suite guests can arrange private beach or deck dinners set up by their butler. Some resorts offer dedicated over-the-water restaurants or clifftop dining. These elevated experiences are available but may carry a small upcharge at select locations.

Beaches Kids Dining

At Beaches Resorts, kids menus, pizza parlors, ice cream shops, and Sesame Street character dining are included. Parents can dine at the same gourmet restaurants adults love, and kids have their own favorites. Families eat well — together or separately.

☕

24-Hour Room Service

Room service is included at all Sandals & Beaches resorts — available around the clock. Butler Suite guests have expanded in-room dining options arranged personally by their butler. No charge, no delivery fee, no tip expected.

🍹

Local Caribbean Cuisine

Most Sandals resorts have at least one dedicated Caribbean restaurant celebrating local flavors — jerk chicken, ackee & saltfish, fresh seafood, and local rum drinks. Experiencing authentic island cuisine is one of the highlights of a Caribbean trip.

Beyond the Resort

Excursions & Adventures

While you could spend your entire trip without leaving the resort — and many guests do — the Caribbean islands offer some incredible off-resort experiences worth considering.

🌊

Dunn's River Falls (Jamaica)

One of Jamaica's most iconic experiences — climbing a natural waterfall in Ocho Rios with a guide. A bucket-list activity that's easily arranged through the resort. Wet and adventurous — leave valuables behind.

🐬

Dolphin Cove (Jamaica)

Swim with dolphins, sharks, and stingrays at this wildlife park in Ocho Rios. Great for families at Beaches. Can be combined with Dunn's River Falls for a full-day adventure.

🤿

Coral Reef Diving & Snorkeling

While snorkeling gear is included with your resort stay, guided reef excursions take you to the best spots around the island. Turks & Caicos and St. Lucia offer some of the Caribbean's most spectacular reef systems.

🌿

Island Tours & Culture

Bob Marley Museum in Kingston, rum factory tours, spice plantation tours in Grenada, local market visits — each island has a rich culture worth exploring. Ask me about the best tours for your specific destination.

🚤

Catamaran & Sunset Cruises

Popular evening activity — a sailing catamaran with open bar, snorkeling, and sunset views. Available from most island locations. Romantic and memorable. Book through the resort excursion desk.

🛵

ATV & Zipline Adventures

Jamaica in particular offers excellent ATV tours through the mountains and jungle zip-line adventures. Best for active couples or teenagers at Beaches. Muddy, fun, and unforgettable.

8 Pro Tips for Sandals & Beaches

1

Book early to lock in the best price — then watch for upgrades. Sandals prices are dynamic and often go up as availability shrinks. Book early, lock in your price, then work with me to monitor for promotions or free upgrade offers closer to your travel date.

2

Ask about current promotions before you book anything. Sandals runs regular promos — free nights, room upgrades, resort credits, free excursions. These aren't always advertised broadly. As your advisor, I know what's available and can apply them to your booking at no extra cost to you.

3

Consider the Butler upgrade for honeymoons. If there's one trip in your life to splurge on a Butler Suite, your honeymoon is it. Having someone personally handle every detail — from setting up your champagne on the beach to making dinner reservations — is an experience unlike anything else.

4

For Beaches with kids, Turks & Caicos is worth the extra cost. Beaches Turks & Caicos is more expensive than the Jamaica properties — but Grace Bay Beach is extraordinary, the resort is stunning, and it's consistently voted the world's best family resort. If budget allows, go there first.

5

Don't skip the scuba diving. It's included. Even if you've never dived before, the beginner resort dive is a guided, shallow, incredibly safe experience — and it's one of the most memorable things you can do in the Caribbean. Hundreds of guests do their first dive at Sandals every week.

6

Arrive early — your room may be ready. Most Sandals resorts have early check-in available if your room is ready, even before the official 3 PM check-in. If you land at noon, head to the resort and ask. Butler Suite guests get priority check-in regardless of arrival time.

7

Always purchase travel protection. Hurricane season is real. Flights get cancelled. Medical emergencies happen. Sandals' travel protection plan or a comparable third-party plan is non-negotiable for international travel. I always recommend it and help clients choose the right coverage level.

8

You don't have to choose between islands alone. This is exactly what I'm here for. Tell me who's going, your budget range, what matters most to you (beach quality, nightlife, adventure, romance, family), and I'll narrow it down to the two or three resorts that best match your family. Then we book the best available deal.
