About Me

The Imagineer Dad

I'm a father of three and a lifelong Disney fan, and I've experienced the magic of multiple Disney vacations with my own family. I know firsthand how exciting — and sometimes overwhelming — planning a theme park trip can be, which is why I'm here to help.

As a travel advisor with Wish Upon a Castle, I stay up-to-date on the latest Disney and Universal tips by watching YouTube guides, reading park news, and connecting with other Disney experts. I love Disney — the magic, the attention to detail, the unforgettable experiences — and I want to make sure every family I work with gets to experience that same joy, stress-free.

I take care of all the details, from dining reservations to park strategies, so you can focus on making memories together.
